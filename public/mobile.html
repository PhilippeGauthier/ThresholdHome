Measure twice. Cut once.

Introducing Threshold 2.0. Built for both the iPad and iPhone.

We’ve completely rebuilt our iOS app from the ground up. Now you can view all of your Threshold photos right from the app as well as upload to your project.

The new Threshold experience is going to drastically reduce the time it takes to view photos while on the jobsite as well as enable your entire project team to monitor progress remotely.

Project View

View every photo ever taken on your project from anywhere. We’ll show you the most recent image taken for each level of navigation so you can always stay up to speed. Drill down by project, floor, and room.

Photo Management

Take photos from within the app or use images already on your device. When you’re ready to upload to the Threshold just choose your location details and hit send. There’s no internet connection required to document the jobsite.

Comments

You can comment on photos before you upload or after the fact. Keep the conversation running where your photos are stored so you’ll always be able to reference the issue.

Search

All of Threshold’s navigation is searchable from within the app. Projects with huge number of floors and rooms can be searched and sorted in seconds.

Background Upload

We’ve taken the process of uploading and moved it to the background. This means that you can upload photos without Wfi and still continue to work.
