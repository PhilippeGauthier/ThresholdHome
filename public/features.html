The best tools for the job(site)

Speed = Simplicity x Efficiency
Threshold combines innovative user interfaces with powerful tools to give you an unparalleled user experience.

  • Dynamic View

    Drag and drop your photos right on to your plans. We'll add the floor, room, and trade data for you.
  • Photo Gallery

    Find that critical photo in seconds. You can search by tags, date, location, or anything else you can think of.
  • News Feed

    Now you or anyone on your project team can get up to speed in seconds. See the photos you care about without searching.
  • Tagging Tools

    Use our trade tags to specify the contents of the photo or use custom tags to isolate specific attributes or issues.

Security done right.

Admin vs. View-only

Threshold brings an innovative and simple approach to user-rights. You can add admins with full control or view-only users who can view photos but aren't allowed to delete or adjust your project.

Public vs. Private

Threshold allows you to make your photos public or private. This means you can always document everything and not have to worry about who sees what.

Redundant Data

Threshold stores your data on Amazon S3's cloud infrastructure. Your mission critical photos are always saved on multiple devices across multiple facilities.

Organize

Keep all of your project photos in one secure online location. Every photo taken will be secure and organized instantly.

Collaborate

Threshold gives you innovative ways to share your project photos and there's no limit to the number of project team members you can add.

Standardize

When your entire project team uses threshold there's never any confusion about how to organize or find a photo.

Simplify

Organizing your photos is as simple as dragging and dropping them on to your plans or uploading them with our mobile app.
