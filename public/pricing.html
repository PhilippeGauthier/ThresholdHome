FAQs

How does archiving work?

Archiving allows users to keep their completed project's online without having to pay for our full feature set.

Can I download my data?

Not yet, but soon. In the next few months we'll give users an option to download their project data.

Can I transfer my project to another user?

You can transfer the project to any Threshold user connected to your project.

What browsers do you support?

We support all browsers including Internet Explorer 9 and above. We suggest for the best user experience you use chrome, safari, or firefox.

What mobile devices do you support?

Initially we're developing for Apple's iOS operating system. You can use your iPhone, iPad, or iPod touch. In the future we'll be developing for Android as well.

How can I pay?

We accept all major credit cards. We'll send you an email receipt with each billing cycle.
