About Threshold

383 Studio, Inc is the maker of Threshold. We're a privately held software company, founded by Adam Freeman and Philippe Gauthier.

We believe in creating software that helps bring an old and antiquated industry into the 21st century. The construction industry has plenty of bloated software packages that are difficult to learn, expensive to implement, and restrictive in their use.

We're creating great software that's inexpensive, intuitive, and easy to share.
