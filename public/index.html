Threshold Shutting Down

Threshold will be shutting down on Dec 30, 2013.

We started this company with the goal of creating a new and improved workflow for the building industry's photo management needs. To that point we believe we succeeded.

Unfortunately, we weren't able to raise outside funding to help us further grow the product. We want to thank our investors who gave us a chance to build Threshold. But most importantly we want to thank you, our customers. Without your support and subscriptions none of this would have been possible.

If you're an existing user read below for instructions on how to download your project

And again, thank you for your support. Please send any questions or comments to feedback@thresholdcm.com.

Best,
The Threshold Team

Project Downloads

  • Downloads are only available to project admins. Please contact the owner of the project if you need to download your project.
  • If you request a project download we'll send you a zip file with both your photos and plans organized by floor and room via email. This link can be forwarded and used multiple times.
  • To download your project you must have a paid project. You can create a free paid project by entering credit card information and using the coupon code Threshold.This is good for 100% off any project.
  • Project downloads will be available until Dec 29th, 2013.
  • Please don't request a project download unless you need one. Creating the files takes a toll on our servers and can create issues for other users.
  • If you don't see the Archive / Download option you need to upgrade to a paid project (see information above).
  • To download your project go to your project's settings and click Archive / Download Project

Take your jobsite back.

Threshold is lightweight project management focused on making photo documentation easier.

Our goal is to empower the building industry to create professional-grade documentation on their own terms.

All Cameras Welcome

Use our iOS app at the jobsite or use any photo on your desktop. Threshold enables you to be backwards compatible and organize both new and old projects.

A Better Way to Communicate

Threshold was meant to be social. You can invite your entire project team to view, organize, and comment on your photos. Keep one centralized photo collection for your entire project.

Location Based Organization

You build your projects with building plans. Why don't you organize your photos that way? With Threshold you can. Assign photos to geographic spaces and never lose another photo again.

Security First

With Threshold you have granular controls over who sees what. In addtion, all your mission-critical photos are backed up across multiple devices in multiple locations.
