Documenting your jobsite is easy as 1-2-3.

Step One: Transfer your photos

Upload to Threshold with our mobile app or use our web application to upload your digital camera photos.

And with our mobile app you can organize and add privacy settings to your photos before you upload.

Step Two: View your photos

Walk your jobsite virtually, scroll through a news feed of recent project photos, or find a specific project photo in seconds.

Threshold uses your plans to organize your photos. In addition, we use industry-specific tags and encourage you to use your own custom tags to take organization even further.

Step Three: Become a hero

Amaze your project team and clients with unparalleled access while maintaining strict control over who sees what.

With powerful user management tools you can give everyone on the project the right level of access to Threshold and your project. And there's no limit to the number of users per project.

And at the end of your project download all your photos and plans or keep them online with us.
